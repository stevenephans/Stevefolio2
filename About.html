Hey I'm Steve and thanks for checking out my portfolio! I'm currently finishing my Master's in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on product design at CU Boulder (graduating May 2021) and am looking for exciting opportunities in engineering product development. I've been a prolific tinkerer as long as I can remember, and thrive in the creative yet technical process of working out novel solutions to tricky design problems.

My strengths include advanced CAD & surface modeling skills (SolidWorks), hands-on prototype development & testing, and 2+ years of design for manufacturing consulting work for injection-molded and CNC machined parts. I've also scripted my fair share of MATLAB code! In addition to these core-competencies, I have a solid foundation in circuit design, signal processing, and a variety of programming languages.

Outside of the design & build process, you'll find me partaking in any number of Colorado's cliche activities: rock climbing, skiing, backpacking, and so on.
